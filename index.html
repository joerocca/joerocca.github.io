About Me

Hello. My name is Joe. I am a young iOS developer who is constantly itching to learn more about the industry. I have always had an affinity for programming, but I was never sure whether or not it was something I would be suitable for. I decided to take the risk and pursue my passion. In February 2015, I joined The Iron Yard. I studied Mobile Engineering and it was all I could possibly ask for. Every minute of my time was occupied by programming and I loved every second of it. I was able to pursue a passion that I have always loved. I am now interested in expanding my skills and consistently increasing my knowledge in all areas relevant.

Education

The Iron Yard

Mobile Engineering • May 2015

The Iron Yard is a 12-week immersive code school that taught me the skills to become a Mobile Developer. I learned how to use Xcode, Git, Objective-C, and a good portion of Swift. During the first 9 weeks, my classmates and I attended lectures from 9am-12pm, then completed a project by the following morning. The final 3 weeks we worked on a project of our choosing. I created Bagel Runner, an endless runner game which I presented at The Iron Yard's Demo Day.
